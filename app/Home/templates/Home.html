How does it work?

  • Snap a Picture of Your Notes

    Take a clear photo of your handwritten notes using your phone. Our app will process the image and extract the text for you.

  • Organize Your Notes

    The extracted text is neatly converted into organized digital lists. Edit, categorize, and save them instantly in the app.

  • Access Anytime, Anywhere

    Your digital notes are securely saved and accessible anytime, whether you're at work, school, or on the go.
